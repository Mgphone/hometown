About Mogok

Mogok is famous for its gemstones, particularly the rare and exquisite rubies. Nestled in Myanmar, it is often called the “Valley of Rubies.” It offers stunning landscapes and rich cultural heritage.

History of Mogok

Mogok has a long history dating back to the 15th century when it was first discovered as a source of high-quality gemstones. The Mogok mines have attracted gem traders from all over the world for centuries.

Sights to See in Mogok

Ruby Mines: Explore the source of the world’s finest rubies.

Mogok Pagodas: Visit the pagodas overlooking the valleys.

Gemstone Market: Experience the lively trading of precious stones.
